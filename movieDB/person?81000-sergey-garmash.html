Sergey Garmash

Biography

Sergei Leonidovich Garmash (b. September 1, 1958, Kherson, Ukraine) is a Soviet and Russian film and stage actor of Ukrainian origin. He is a People's Artist of Russia. Description above from the Wikipedia article Sergei Garmash, licensed under CC-BY-SA, full list of contributors on Wikipedia.
