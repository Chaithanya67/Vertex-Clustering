Roschdy Zem

Biography

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Roschdy Zem (born 1965-09-27, Gennevilliers, Hauts-de-Seine) is a French actor of Moroccan descent. He shared the award for Best Actor for his role in the film Days of Glory at the 2006 Cannes Film Festival.
