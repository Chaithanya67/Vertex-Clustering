Stella Stevens

Biography

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Stella Stevens (born October 1, 1936 as Estelle Caro Eggleston) is an American film, television and stage actress, who began her acting career in 1959. She is a film producer, director and pin-up girl.
