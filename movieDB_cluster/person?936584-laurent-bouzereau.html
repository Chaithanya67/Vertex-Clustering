Laurent Bouzereau

Biography

Laurent Bouzereau is a French-American award-winning documentary filmmaker, producer, and best-selling author. He is best-known for his home video/movie documentary work. His name has appeared in the closing credits for over 200 "making-of" documentaries and featurettes.
