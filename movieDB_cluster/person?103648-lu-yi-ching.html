Lu Yi-Ching

Biography

Lu Yi-ching (Chinese: 陸弈靜, born 23 October 1958) is a Taiwanese film and television actress. She played in several films directed by Tsai Ming-liang such as The Wayward Cloud, What Time Is It There? and films by Cheng Wen-tang, including Blue Cha Cha.
