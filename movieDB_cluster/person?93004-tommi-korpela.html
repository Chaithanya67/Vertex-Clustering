Tommi Korpela

Biography

Tommi Korpela is a Finnish actor and screenwriter. Best known for his roles in Man's Job (2007), Rare Exports: A Christmas Tale (2010), Distractions (2015) and Eternal Road (2017). He has won Jussi Award (Finnish equivalent of the Oscars) for Best Actor three times in 2008, 2009 and 2015.

Korpela graduated from Helsinki Theatre Academy in 1995.
