Ranking for Computer Science in Italy

The ranking is based on h-index, citations and number of DBLP documents gathered by May 16th 2020

This ranking includes all top computer scientists affiliated with Italy. There is a total of 183 scholars included with 16 of them also being features in the global ranking. The total sum for the H-index values for leading scientists in Italy is 9314 with a mean value for the h-index of 50.90. The total sum for the DBLP publications for leading scientists in Italy is 38958 with a mean value for DBLP publications is 212.89. The highest number of scientists is affiliated with University of Bologna (21 scientists).

Please consider that the country associated with a scientist is based on their affiliated research institution as featured on Google Scholar, not on their real nationality.
