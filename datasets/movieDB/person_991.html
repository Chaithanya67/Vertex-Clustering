Olivia Colman

Biography

A graduate of the Bristol Old Vic Theatre School, Sarah Caroline Olivia Sinclair CBE, known professionally as Olivia Colman, is an English actress and narrator. She is the recipient of numerous accolades, including an Academy Award for Best Actress, four BAFTA Awards, three Golden Globe Awards, four British Independent Film Awards, a Screen Actors Guild Award, a Volpi Cup, and a BFI Fellowship.
