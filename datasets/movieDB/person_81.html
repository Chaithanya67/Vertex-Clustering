Yuusuke Kobayashi

Biography

Yūsuke Kobayashi is a Japanese voice actor. He is affiliated with Yu-rin Pro. In 2017, he won the Best New Actor Award at the 11th Seiyu Awards. He was born in Tokyo, and is 1.64 m tall.
